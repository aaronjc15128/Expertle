EXPERTLE










Uses almost 10x less English 5-letter words for solutions.

Note: This does not affect guesses, you will be able to guess any valid 5-letter word.

Classic Wordle!

Note: Some words used in the original Wordle game are not used in this game due to the words being either highly unknown or generally just not fun to play with.

Classic Wordle Hard Mode.

Yellows & Greens will display to the side of the word.

This means that the letters that are Yellow/Green will not show and instead only the total number of Yellows/Greens in the word will show.

Guess the EXPERTLE in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.
Hit the enter button to submit. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


Examples


The letter W is in the word and in the correct spot.


The letter I is in the word but in the wrong spot.


The letter U is not in the word in any spot.
